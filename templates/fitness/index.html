Fitness Tips

Simple ways to improve your fitness

Daily Movement

Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate physical activity every day.

Strength Training

Include strength training exercises at least twice a week to build muscle.

Proper Rest

Get 7-9 hours of quality sleep to allow your body to recover and grow.

Cardio Health

Regular cardiovascular exercise improves heart health and endurance.

Workout Plans

Choose a plan that fits your goals

Weight Loss

A combination of cardio and strength training to help you burn fat and build lean muscle.

  • 3-4 days of cardio
  • 2-3 days of strength training
  • Focus on full-body workouts

Muscle Gain

Heavy resistance training with proper nutrition to maximize muscle growth and strength.

  • 4-5 days of strength training
  • Split routines for different muscle groups
  • Progressive overload principle

General Fitness

A balanced approach to improve overall health, endurance, and functional fitness.

  • 2-3 days of cardio
  • 2-3 days of strength training
  • 1-2 days of flexibility/mobility work
